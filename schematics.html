NASA Schematics Catalog

Finding critical spacecraft information on launch day

On launch day, there might be problems with the spacecraft that engineers must resolve quickly. If they can't resolve the issues in time, the launch might get delayed or scrubbed entirely. It's important that engineers have everything they need at their fingertips. They'll refer to rocket schematics to diagnose issues, but those schematics are currently stored in thick binders. Rather than hunt and pick through several enormous binders, we're working on a search interface so engineers can quickly find exactly what they need. By spending less time searching and more time problem solving, we can ensure a timely and safe launch.
